Our AI engine uses Google Gemini to semantically match your profile to live job listings — going beyond keyword search to understand context, seniority, and role fit.

When shortlisted, receive an AI-generated interview via your dashboard. Answer questions one by one — Gemini scores each answer in real time and generates a full performance analysis.
